My Favourite Book: The Alchemist

About the Book

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a timeless classic about a shepherd boy from Spain who discovers his Personal Legend, sets out on a journey to fulfill it,
and learns valuable lessons about himself and the world along the way.

Why I love it

  • It is engaging and captivating narrating story.
  • The story is filled with interesting characters and unexpected plot twists.
  • Apart from other self-help or spiritual books; it's a fictional story,but also motivates and inspires us.

About the Author

Paulo Coelho is a Brazilian novelist. He is best known for writing The Alchemist, but many of his other books have become best sellers around the world.
His work has been translated into 83 languages.
